The adventure in the depths of the ocean begins!

Are you ready, Sharkie? You’re not just a cute little shark – you’re fast, determined, and ready to stir things up in the ocean.

But beware, dangers lurk in the deep:

Coins – Collect as many as you can! They’ll be useful later on. But watch out: your capacity is limited. Pufferfish – They may look harmless, but get too close and their spikes shoot out – and bam: damage! Jellyfish – Living electric bombs. A single shock will drain your precious health. And then he awaits you… at the edge of the abyss: the Monster Whale – massive, furious, and nearly unbeatable. > Air bubbles? Barely scratch him, just 1% damage. > Poison bubbles? They hit much harder – a full 10%!

Controls

: Swim right : Swim left : Swim up : Swim down : Shoot air bubbles : Shoot poison bubbles : Fin strike

PS: Collect, dodge, aim true – and face your destiny. The deep sea has been waiting for you.
